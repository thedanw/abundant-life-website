### How to Get Started — 5-Step Onboarding

| Step | What Happens | Timeline |
|------|--------------|----------|
| **1. Explore** | Attend a Sunday service, check out the teams, pray about where to serve | Weeks 1–4 |
| **2. Apply** | Complete the short application form below | Week 5 |
| **3. Connect** | Meet with a ministry leader to discuss your gifts and availability | Week 6 |
| **4. Train** | Role-specific training (Blue Card application if needed) | Weeks 7–8 |
| **5. Serve** | Join your team and start making a difference! | Week 9+ |

**No experience required for most roles.** We provide training, mentoring, and ongoing support.
